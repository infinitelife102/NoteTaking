# Privacy – NoteTaking

NoteTaking does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

- **Data storage**: All notes and app data are stored only on your device (or in your browser for the web build).
- **Encryption**: Note content is encrypted locally using a key stored in your device’s secure storage (e.g. Keychain / Keystore).
- **No analytics**: The app does not include analytics or tracking.
- **Offline**: The app is designed to work fully offline; no account or internet connection is required.

If you use cloud backup (e.g. iCloud, Google Drive) for your device, your backup may include the app’s data according to your backup settings.
